A young girl, fascinated by the unsolved murder case known as the “Yuvisona Killer” sets out to find the whereabouts of the serial killer. finally she found his abandoned house in the deep forest, She come inside and found discovers a mysterious diary that tells the story of a man known as “The Rainy Green Man”, who saved the life of a lawyer who had once helped him escape a case ten years ago. He wishes to leave his past behind and start a new life with her, hoping she can heal his twisted mind. Unaware... that the karma from his past deeds has already begun to unfold. Therefore, she must search for more clues in the house to piece together the story using the diary and the case files, in order to uncover the truth and the real identity of “The Rainy Green Man”
