Welcome to my website!

“CRAZYCITY2013 or CC2013” is the pen name I use. Because it's from the first full-length comic I wrote in 2013 and because the characters I create tend to be full of crazy people and weird people. I like to draw comics that have violent and pathetic content, but the secondary content that I draw tends to be cute and heartwarming as well.

I like to create imperfect characters. Every character I create has to have one side that people like and one side that people don't like. It's like 50% good 50% evil. I aim to make people who like my characters accept their evil side. I want you to like my characters because you feel good and relate to them more than because that character is a good person and has a good mind. If you feel good about being attached to them, you will do anything for them. they No matter how many attitudes they have that you don't like.
